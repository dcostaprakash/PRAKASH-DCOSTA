Favourite Food

Just typing Favourite Food make my tastebuds tingle!
Being a foodie, its a tough decision to choose which is my favourite food.
Ofcourse it also depends on the time one is eating.
Especially if it is any one of the following:

  • Breakfast
  • Lunch
  • Dinner

I have mentioned one of my favourites in each category (It was a hard choice though)
I am sure you this will make you drool..

Eggs for Breakfast

It mostly starts with a delicious serving of poached eggs.
The day is incomplete without it.
It goes really well with some bacon or sausages!






Veggies for Lunch

I tend to keep it light during the day with some grilled exotic vegetables.
Must include are mushrooms, brocolli, carrots and Zuchini.
Its simple to make but delicious all the way!





A Delicious aromatic Biryani for Dinner

Well an ideal day ends with a large portion of Biryani.
Its an Indian dish with highly seasoned rice and meat.
But without a dash of curd, it is incomplete!
